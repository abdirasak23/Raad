FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our POS system

How do I set up my POS system for the first time?

Setting up your POS system is quick and easy! After logging in for the first time, our guided setup wizard will walk you through the essential steps: configuring your business information, adding products to your inventory, setting up payment methods, and connecting your hardware. The entire process typically takes 15-20 minutes. Our support team is available 24/7 if you need any assistance during setup.

What payment methods does your POS system support?

Our POS system supports all major payment methods including credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), contactless payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), mobile wallets, and cash transactions. We also integrate with popular payment processors and offer split payment options, allowing customers to pay using multiple methods in a single transaction.

Can I manage inventory across multiple locations?

Absolutely! Our cloud-based POS system allows you to manage inventory across unlimited locations from a single dashboard. You can track stock levels in real-time, transfer inventory between locations, set location-specific pricing, and generate consolidated reports. The system automatically syncs data across all your stores, ensuring you always have accurate inventory information.

Is my business data secure with your POS system?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption (256-bit SSL) for all data transmission and storage. Our system is PCI-DSS compliant, ensuring your customers' payment information is protected. We perform regular security audits, maintain automated backups, and offer role-based access controls. Your data is stored in secure cloud servers with 99.9% uptime guarantee.

What kind of customer support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including live chat, email, and phone. Our dedicated support team consists of POS experts who can help with technical issues, training, and best practices. We also provide extensive documentation, video tutorials, and a knowledge base. Premium support plans include priority assistance and dedicated account managers.
