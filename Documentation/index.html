Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with RAAD POS in just a few minutes. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit our registration page and sign up with your business email. You'll receive a verification link to activate your account.

Step 2: Configure Basic Settings

Set up your store information, including:

  • Store name and contact details
  • Business hours and timezone
  • Tax rates and currency preferences
  • Receipt templates and branding

Step 3: Add Your Products

Import your product catalog using our CSV template or add items manually. Each product can include:

  • Product name, SKU, and barcode
  • Pricing and tax categories
  • Stock quantities and variants
  • Product images and descriptions
// Example API endpoint for adding products POST https://api.raadpos.com/v1/products Content-Type: application/json { "name": "Premium Coffee Beans", "sku": "COF-001", "price": 24.99, "stock": 150, "category": "Beverages" }

Step 4: Start Selling

Access the POS interface and begin processing transactions. Use the search function to quickly find products and complete sales.

System Requirements

Ensure your hardware and software meet the minimum requirements for optimal performance:

Hardware Requirements

  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent (i5 recommended)
  • RAM: Minimum 4GB (8GB recommended)
  • Storage: 10GB available space for installation
  • Display: 1280x720 resolution minimum
  • Internet: Stable broadband connection (5 Mbps minimum)

Compatible Devices

  • Desktop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
  • Touch screen monitors
  • Barcode scanners (USB or Bluetooth)
  • Receipt printers (thermal or impact)
  • Cash drawers with compatible interface

Sales Management

Our sales management tools help you process transactions quickly and efficiently while maintaining accurate records.

Processing a Sale

  1. Scan or search for products to add to the cart
  2. Apply any discounts or promotions
  3. Select the payment method (cash, card, mobile payment)
  4. Complete the transaction and print or email the receipt

Payment Methods

Accept payments through multiple channels:

  • Cash transactions with change calculation
  • Credit and debit card processing
  • Mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
  • Split payments across multiple methods
  • Store credit and gift cards

Returns and Refunds

Process returns seamlessly with our built-in refund system. Track reasons for returns to identify product issues or opportunities for improvement.
