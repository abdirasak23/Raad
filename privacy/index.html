Introduction

Welcome to Raad ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the Raad mobile application (the "App"), a Point of Sale (POS) "Smart Business Book" designed to help small and medium businesses manage their sales, inventory, and customer credit (Dayn).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Key Principle: Your business data is yours. We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our services. We never sell your personal or business data to third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our App:

1.1 Information You Provide

Data TypeDescription
Account Information Your name, email address, password, and (optionally) phone number when you create an account.
Google Account If you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email, and profile picture as provided by Google.
Business Information Business name, location, currency, and (optionally) your business logo.
Referral Code If you are referred by another user, we collect the referral code you enter.

1.2 Your Business Data (You Own This)

This is the data you generate while using the app. We store this data on your behalf to provide our services.

Data TypeDescription
Product Information Product names, prices, stock quantities, barcodes, and product images.
Customer Information Your customers' names, phone numbers, and their credit (Dayn) balances.
Sales & Transaction Data Records of your sales, orders, expenses, and transaction history.
Staff Accounts Information for any staff members you add to your account.

1.3 Device & Technical Data

Data TypeDescription
Device Information Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
Usage Analytics Information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, taps, and crash reports. This helps us fix bugs.

1.4 App Permissions

Our App requests specific permissions to enable key features:

PermissionWhy We Need It
Camera To allow you to scan product barcodes to manage inventory and make sales.
Bluetooth To connect to and print receipts on Bluetooth thermal printers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

To create your account, provide authentication, and secure your access.

To provide the core features of the App: processing sales, managing inventory, and tracking customer credit (Dayn).

To securely back up and sync your business data to our cloud backend (AWS).

To enable barcode scanning via your device's camera.

To connect to your Bluetooth receipt printer.

To calculate your Level and XP for our referral program.

To communicate with you, respond to support requests, and send service updates.

To analyze usage data and crash reports to fix bugs, improve app stability, and enhance features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We use third-party service providers to help us operate. These providers are only given the information they need to perform their service.

  • AWS: Our backend provider for database hosting, cloud storage, and authentication. They store all your business data securely.
  • Google: To provide Google Sign-In as an authentication option.
  • Analytics Services: To help us understand app usage and fix crashes.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process.

Data Storage & Security

The security of your data is our top priority. Your data is stored and secured by our backend provider, **AWS**.

Your data is stored in an encrypted database.

All data sent between your app and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS.

We use secure authentication methods, including email/password and Google Sign-In, to protect your account.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no electronic storage method is 100% secure. We continuously work to enhance our security practices.

Our App is a business tool and is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate their account.
