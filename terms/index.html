Offline Data and Syncing

Raad POS is designed to work seamlessly in offline mode, ensuring your business never stops.

How Offline Mode Works

Data recorded while offline is stored locally on your device

Data automatically syncs to our cloud servers (Supabase) when internet connection is restored

All your transactions remain accessible even without internet

Important Data Sync Warnings

Device Loss: If you lose your device or uninstall the app before connecting to the internet, any unsynced data may be permanently lost.

Data Responsibility: Raad POS is not liable for data lost due to device failure, theft, damage, or failure to sync.

Best Practice: We strongly recommend connecting to the internet regularly to ensure your data is backed up to the cloud.
